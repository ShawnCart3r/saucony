Automaster Shelburne Half Marathon & 5k/10k
Date
Sunday, May 3rd
Location
Shelburne, VT 05482
Beginning and ending at Shelburne Field House, the half marathon is a loop course in picturesque Shelburne, Vermont. You'll run on a combination of dirt and back roads including a portion on the Meach Cove property, past Shelburne Farms, and along Harbor Road with beautiful views of Lake Champlain and the Adirondacks. The 5K and 10K are both out-and-back courses along Harbor Road — the 5K is relatively flat, while the 10K has a couple of hills.
